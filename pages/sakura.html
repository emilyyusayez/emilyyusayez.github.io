3D cinematic using the Unreal Engine 4 cameras and cinematic effects with open-source assets.

This project was developed as part of an Interactive Development class project. It required the use of Unreal Engine 4 to create a cinematic video, such as a game trailer or 3D teaser. I utilized pre-made assets and scenes available on the Unreal Engine Marketplace, while the characters and their animations were sourced from Mixamo.
