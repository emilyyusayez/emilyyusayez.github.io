3D modeling of a room using Maya Software focusing on the modeling, lighting and texturing aspects.

This project was developed for the end-of-year examination in the 3D modeling course. The primary aim of this exercise was to transform a detailed room illustration into a 3D representation. Each object was meticulously recreated and tailored to seamlessly integrate into the new scene. Lighting choices, including both a nighttime version and a dawn version, were also carefully adjusted.
